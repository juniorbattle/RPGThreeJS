# GPT external prompts — Elite monsters v1

Destination for generated raw images:

`public/assets/characters/pixel/validation/gpt-elite-monsters-v1/raw/`

Purpose:

This batch is for demo elite encounters only. These sprites should feel stronger than basic monsters and generic Serpent units, but they must not look like final bosses unless explicitly stated.

Rules:

- Generate one image per prompt.
- Do not generate a spritesheet or atlas.
- Use a solid `#FF00FF` magenta background only.
- No scenery, no text, no UI, no ground plate, no cast shadow.
- Full body visible, centered, safe margins around weapon, wings, horns and feet.
- Bright readable tactical palette; avoid sprites that become too dark on forest or burning village battle scenes.
- Avoid pure black except tiny accents.
- Add subtle warm rim light and clean highlights.
- Style target: stylized pixel art fantasy tactical RPG, coherent with the current hero sprites.
- Canvas: 1086x1448 or 1024x1365.

## Shared negative prompt

Do not include: realistic rendering, painterly background, environment, text, labels, watermark, UI, multiple characters, multiple poses, sprite atlas, white background, transparent checkerboard, cropped body, cut off feet, excessive darkness, gore, final boss scale, giant 2x2 board monster unless requested, modern clothing, sci-fi armor.
